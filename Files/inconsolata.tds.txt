Inconsolata regular and bold weight, enhanced with more glyphs, and a support package offering options to control the style of the zero (default is slashed), the shape of quotes and the shape of lower-case L. Version 1.04 includes otf versions that work better with LuaLaTeX and XeLaTeX than those from earlier versions, and includes new fixes to some fd files, as well as changes to some glyph names that function better when copying from pdf to ASCII.
